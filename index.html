What's a hackathon?

A hackathon is the single largest thing to happen to education since the Internet. Students from across the world come to MHacks to build amazing things, turn visions into products, and to meet other people energized about the same things as you. MHacks isn't just for hardcore programmers. It's not just about competing. It isn't about energy-drink-enabled sleep deprivation. It isn't about starting a viable business. MHacks is for creators. It's for those of you who see problems and want to tackle them, using existing skills, and augmenting them with the skills of those around you.

What is the registration process?

Filling out our registration application will give us a sense of who you are, and what you’re capable of. We’re not looking for just the best hackers, but also for those who are willing to do what it takes to bring an idea to fruition, and learn the entire time. Within a few weeks, you’ll receive word on whether you’ll be coming to MHacks.

What if I don’t have an idea?

No problem! Many hackers arrive at a hackathon with little to no idea of what they’ll be building over the next 36 hours. Check out our API expo to see what amazing tools are available to you as a result of our sponsors and talk to your team about all of the opportunities that exist. Aim for a prize, or don’t. That is totally up to you.

What if I don’t have a team?

A few weeks before MHacks, we’ll connect ticketed hackers with a database of other available hackers looking for a member for their team, or looking to create one. You’ll list your skills, GitHubs, and contact information. If this is your first hackathon, there will definitely be others who don’t have prior experience that are looking for team members, so don’t worry.

Is it expensive? I'm broke.

As a result of our committed sponsors, MHacks is free for undergraduates and high school students from across the world. Don’t fall into this category, contact us for how you can get involved.

What can I hack on?

First off, bring your laptop and smartphone. Additionally, we’ll be providing lots of awesome hardware to hack on courtesy of our sponsors. Stay tuned to this page and MHacks social media for updates on specifics.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, smartphone, charging accessories, Student ID, sleeping bag if you have one, a change of clothes, toiletries, and a well-rested open mind. Leave it to us to blow your mind.

How will I get there?

After your registration is confirmed and you receive word that you’re coming to MHacks, we’ll share information about buses, travel reimbursements, and check-in.

Are there travel reimbursements?

We’re willing to reimburse up to $300, depending on your location, availability of transportation and experience. We recommend you complete the application first, and after hearing back from us, consider travel logistics.

Can I just stop in to see the expo?

Yes. We’ll be sharing more information about the public expo soon. Please do not fill out the MHacks registration application.
